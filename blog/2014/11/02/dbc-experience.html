DBC Experience

Posted on: 11/02/2014 Read Time: 5 Minutes

My thoughts

I like the DBC experience. It's unique because, it's split up into a remote phase and an on-site phases. The remote phase allows students to sharpen their programming. When arrive on-site, students are better programmers and logical thinkers.

I also think that focusing on engineering with empathy is awesome because it teaches one how to be a well-balanced person in life.

I see myself learning a lot and also hope to share my knowledge about any cool tricks that I may know or figure out. I hope others also share their cool tricks with class as well. My expectations for DBC have not changed. I expect to put as much as I can in so I can get as much as I can out of the program while meeting awsome people at the same time.

I’m excited to push myself everyday. One random takeaway from the program so far is the pomodoro productivty method. I really like it a lot because it’s helping me stay productive so I can learn as much as possible. Also, I can’t wait to meet the NY Cohort in Janaury 2015. Anyways that’s it for now. Until next time.
